A Game of Jewish Knowledge

Jewish IQ is a fast-paced and engaging board game for 2-6 players designed to test and expand knowledge of Jewish history, culture, religion, and traditions. Players move around a Star of David-shaped board, answering trivia questions of increasing difficulty to earn points. The first player to reach the target score and correctly answer a final, high-level question wins!

The Six Categories

The game features six trivia categories, each color-coded to match spaces on the board. Explore the topics you'll be tested on:

Blue (Tanakh)

Questions about the Torah, Prophets (Nevi'im), and Writings (Ketuvim).

Sub-Categories:
  • Genesis, Exodus, Key Figures, Prophets, Psalms

White (Holidays & Shabbat)

Questions about the Jewish calendar, festivals, and the Sabbath.

Sub-Categories:
  • High Holidays, Passover, Hanukkah, Purim, Shabbat Traditions

Red (Lifecycles & Rituals)

Questions about Jewish prayer, laws, and major life events.

Sub-Categories:
  • Kashrut (Kosher), Prayer, Bar/Bat Mitzvah, Jewish Weddings, Mitzvot

Brown (Jewish History)

Questions about Jewish history from the post-Biblical era to the 20th century.

Sub-Categories:
  • Second Temple, The Diaspora, Medieval Period, Haskalah, Famous Personalities

Green (Culture & Language)

Questions about Jewish food, music, art, and languages (Hebrew, Yiddish, Ladino).

Sub-Categories:
  • Yiddish Terms, Jewish Food, Arts & Entertainment, Modern Hebrew

Gold (Modern Israel)

Questions about the geography, history, and culture of the modern State of Israel.

Sub-Categories:
  • Geography, Cities, Modern History, Politics & Culture

How to Play

Rules of Play

  1. Setup: All players place their "Mitzvah Marker" on the "Start" space. Choose a player to go first.
  2. On Your Turn: Roll the die and move your piece that many spaces along the path.
  3. Draw a Card: The player to your left (the "Rabbi" for your turn) draws a card from the deck matching the color of the space you landed on.
  4. Choose Difficulty: The "Rabbi" reads the Category and Sub-Category aloud.
  5. Select Difficulty: You must choose your difficulty level *before* hearing the question: Level 1 (1 pt), Level 2 (3 pts), or Level 3 (5 pts).
  6. Answer the Question: The "Rabbi" reads the corresponding question aloud.
  7. Scoring:
    • If Correct: You receive the points for that level (1, 3, or 5) and add them to your score. Your turn ends.
    • If Incorrect: You receive no points. Your turn ends.
  8. Pass Play: Play passes to the player on the left.

Winning the Game: The Final Mitzvah

  1. Reach the Goal: The game is played to a predetermined amount of points (e.g., 50 points).
  2. Become Eligible: Once you accumulate 50 or more points, you are eligible to win. You do not win immediately.
  3. On Your Next Turn: You must attempt the "Final Mitzvah."
    • You roll and move as normal.
    • You are automatically asked the Level 3 (5-point) question for the space you land on.
    • If you answer correctly: You win the game!
    • If you answer incorrectly: Your turn ends. You must try again on your next turn.
